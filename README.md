# Frankly 🎯

A student-only anonymous perspective sharing platform 
where real people share how the world actually works.
No jobs. No courses. No fake motivation. Just honest 
perspectives.

## The Problem 😔
Students are confused, scared, and have nobody to 
ask real questions to — without judgement.

- Scared to apply for internships
- Confused about degree vs skill
- Tired of fake jobs and scams on LinkedIn
- Introverts with no safe space to speak
- Nobody explaining how the real world works

## What Frankly Is ✅
- Anonymous perspective sharing
- Real career and life discussions
- Safe space for introverts and confused students
- Honest voices from alumni, HRs and founders
- No judgement. No pressure. Just clarity.

## What Frankly Is NOT ❌
- Not a job portal
- Not LinkedIn
- Not a course platform
- No resume sharing
- No fake motivation
- No selling

## Who Is It For 👥
- Confused students
- Introverts
- Students with backlogs
- Students scared to apply
- Students tired of LinkedIn culture

## Core Features 🔧
- Anonymous posting and questions
- Perspective sharing by real people
- Career reality discussions
- Student life conversations
- Budget and resource guidance
